Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our telemedicine services

You can contact our helpline at 1800-TELECARE or visit our website to locate the nearest center. Our centers are strategically placed to serve 2-3 villages within walking distance.

Yes, all our telemedicine services are completely free for rural communities. This initiative is funded by the Government of India and charitable donations to ensure healthcare accessibility for all.

You need to bring your Aadhaar card for patient ID linking, and any previous medical reports or prescriptions. Our CHO will help register you and maintain your digital medical records.

Our specialist doctors can consult on general medicine, pediatrics, cardiology, dermatology, mental health, and many other specialties. For emergencies, we provide immediate support and referrals.

After consultation, the doctor will create a digital prescription linked to your Aadhaar ID. You can access it anytime through our system or get a printed copy from the health center.

Yes, all your medical consultations, prescriptions, and health records are securely stored and linked to your Aadhaar ID. You can access them at any Rural TeleCare center across India.
