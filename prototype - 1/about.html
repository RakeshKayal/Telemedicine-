About Rural TeleCare

Bridging the healthcare divide through innovative telemedicine solutions designed specifically for rural India

Our Mission

Rural TeleCare is revolutionizing healthcare delivery in rural India by connecting remote communities with specialist medical care through cutting-edge telemedicine technology.

We believe that geographical barriers should never prevent anyone from accessing quality healthcare. Our platform bridges the gap between rural patients and urban medical expertise, ensuring that every village has access to the same level of care available in major cities.

How It Works

Our simple 4-step process ensures every rural patient gets connected to the right specialist doctor

1

Visit Health Center

Patient visits the nearest Rural TeleCare center located within 2-3 villages. Our trained Community Health Officers (CHO) are ready to assist.

2

Health Assessment

CHO records vital signs including blood pressure, weight, height, temperature, and discusses symptoms to determine the right specialist.

3

Doctor Connection

Patient is connected to the appropriate specialist doctor via secure video call. CHO facilitates the consultation and provides medical history.

4

Digital Prescription

Doctor provides consultation, diagnosis, and digital prescription linked to patient's Aadhaar ID for future reference and medical history.
